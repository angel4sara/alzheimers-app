Alzheimer’s Detection

Alzheimer's disease is a neurodegenerative disease that develops gradually and progressively worse over time, affecting millions of people. It leads to dementia and eventually causes death as a result of the shutting down of bodily functions.

What are we doing here?

This application uses machine learning (SVM) to analyze brain scan images (MRI). By processing specific image regions and extracting HOG features, our trained model predicts whether the patient shows signs of dementia, mild dementia, or no dementia.
